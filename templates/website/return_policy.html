Return Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, we’re here to help.

Returns

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item within 30 days of receiving it. Please ensure the item is unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition that you received it.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, your item must:

  • Be in its original condition (unused, unworn, and unwashed).
  • Be in its original packaging with all tags and accessories.
  • Be returned within 30 days of the delivery date.

We reserve the right to reject any return that doesn’t meet these criteria.

Non-Returnable Items

Some items are non-returnable, including:

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software or digital products
  • Personalized or custom-made items

How to Return an Item

To return an item, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at contact@cfcsoftware.com or call us at +1 555 123 4567.
  2. Provide your order number, the item(s) you wish to return, and the reason for the return.
  3. Once your return request is approved, we will provide you with a return authorization and return instructions.
  4. Pack the item securely, including the original packaging, and send it to the address provided by our customer service team.

Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange an item for a different size or color, please return the original item and place a new order through our website. Exchanges are subject to availability.

Shipping Costs for Returns

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the item is defective or incorrect. We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance for returns, as we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately at contact@cfcsoftware.com or call us at +1 555 123 4567. We will assist you in processing a return or exchange, and we will cover the cost of return shipping for defective items.

International Returns

For international returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. Customs duties and taxes may apply when returning items from overseas, and the customer is responsible for these charges. Please ensure that you follow any specific international return guidelines outlined by the local authorities.
