1. Introduction

At CFC Software, we strive to provide the best quality services to our customers. However, if you are not satisfied with your purchase or if you wish to cancel or request a refund, please review our Cancellation and Refund Policy below.

2. Cancellation Policy

If you wish to cancel an order, you may do so within 7 days from the date of purchase. To initiate a cancellation, please contact us by email at contact@cfcsoftware.com. If your cancellation is approved, you will receive a full refund as per the guidelines mentioned below.

3. Refund Policy

Our refund policy is designed to ensure that you receive a full refund for eligible purchases. The refund eligibility is as follows:

  • Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase.
  • Refund requests must be made through email at contact@cfcsoftware.com.
  • Refunds will only be issued if the service or product has not been fully delivered or used.
  • Once a refund is processed, the amount will be credited to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.

4. Non-refundable Items

Some products or services are non-refundable, including:

  • Products or services that have been fully delivered and are used.
  • Customized or personalized products or services.
  • Services that have been rendered or partially completed.

5. Process for Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us via email at contact@cfcsoftware.com within 7 days of the purchase.
  2. Provide details about the order, including the order number, product/service details, and reason for cancellation or refund request.
  3. Once your request is received, our customer support team will review your case and process your request according to our policy.

6. Processing Time for Refunds

Refund requests will be processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method, and you will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed.
