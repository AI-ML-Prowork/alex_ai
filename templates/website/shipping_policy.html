Shipping Policy

We are committed to providing you with fast and reliable shipping services. Below are the terms and conditions that govern our shipping policy.

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or holidays. If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery.

Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges for your order will be calculated and displayed at checkout. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the shipping method chosen during checkout.

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • International Shipping: 7-15 business days (depending on the destination)

Shipping Destinations

We offer shipping to addresses within India and select international destinations. For international orders, please note that customs duties and taxes may apply based on your location.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number to monitor the status of your shipment. Please allow up to 24 hours for tracking information to become available.

Shipping Restrictions

Some products may have shipping restrictions based on the destination. If your order contains restricted items, you will be notified, and we may offer alternatives or a refund for the restricted products.

Damaged or Lost Items

If your package is damaged or lost in transit, please contact our customer service team immediately. We will initiate a claim with the carrier and work to resolve the issue. Please retain all packaging and tracking information for claims processing.

International Shipping

For international orders, please note that you are responsible for any additional customs fees, duties, taxes, or other charges that may be incurred during the shipping process. These charges are assessed by your country's customs office and are not included in the price of the product or the shipping cost.

Shipping Timeframes

While we strive to meet delivery estimates, please be aware that shipping timeframes are not guaranteed. Certain factors such as weather conditions, holidays, and customs delays may impact the estimated delivery date.
